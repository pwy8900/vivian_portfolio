Intro

Envite is an innovative web application designed to revolutionize the way we connect with the environment. With a focus on user engagement and accessibility, Envite serves as a comprehensive platform where users can input their data of their lifestyles such as what they used to commute or what they had in a meal and we'll calculate their footprint. We provide a rewarding system to encourage people to join in the action of being more environmental friendly just by changing a bit of their lifestyles.

Features

1. Using Real-time counter to show the amount of CO2 emitted into the atmosphereGlobally this year to raise the public's awareness

2. Users can log into their account to store their data and we will generate a personalized footprint dashboard for them

3. We used API to call the questions of the Trivia game and the users get to test their knowledge and get virtual coins if they answered the questions correctly. They can use the coins for gifts in our online shop
