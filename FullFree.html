Intro

According to Taiwan Business TOPICS, Taiwan generates about 16.5 million tons of food waste every year, which could fill 182 Olympic-siz swimming pools EVERY DAY! We are a dedicated platform aimed at combating food waste by connecting restaurants with consumers. Our mission is to reduce surplus food in the hospitality industry by allowing restaurants to share their leftover meals and products that are nearing expiration dates at significantly reduced prices. By facilitating this exchange, we not only help restaurants minimize waste but also provide customers with affordable dining options. Join us in making a positive impact on our environment while enjoying delicious meals at a fraction of the cost.

Share Food

FullFree allows restaurants to upload information about leftover food in just a few clicks

Instant Update

FullFree provides users with latest update of available food to avoid outdated information and ensures that users do not miss out on new food posts

Reservation System

FullFree enables users to reserve food using a reserve system and ensures users receive the desired number of servings. It also allows restaurant owners to easily manage orders and to ensure that all orders would be picked up
